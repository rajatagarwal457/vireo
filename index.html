How it works

From raw clips to a posted reel in four steps.

No timeline scrubbing, no keyframes. Just bring the footage and talk to Vireo like you'd brief a great editor.

01

Upload your clips

Drag in raw footage from your phone or camera — as many takes as you've got.

02

Describe it + add audio

Say the vibe, length, and platform. Upload a track or tell Vireo the sound you want.

03

Vireo edits the video

It cuts, paces to the beat, adds captions and transitions — a post-ready first draft.

04

Chat to refine

“Punch up the intro,” “cut the dead air,” “swap the hook.” Vireo re-edits on the fly.
